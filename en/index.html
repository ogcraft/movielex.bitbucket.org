We are proud to present MovieLexApp. MovieLexApp is a smartphone app that enables non-English speaking audiences to enjoy original mainstream English speaking movies in theaters and on home TV sets, by allowing them to hear the corresponding audio track translated to their own language, in real time, via headphones connected to their smartphone.

The list of currently available movies you can find here.

How it works?

The user selects the movie from the list provided by application and pick a desired language from available transaltions.
MovieLexApp downloads the relevant movie translation track from our site.

After the movie will start and the user will press the "Start" button. The application uses the smartphone microphone to synchronize the translation audio track with the audio of ongoing movie.

After about of 10-20 seconds the synchronization process will finish and the user can hear translation of movie dialogs in his smartphone headphones.

Try a demo

We offer you a short excerpt from the well known movie and you can check how our system is working.

To do this, please install "MovieLexApp" on your smartphone, select "MovieLex demo" movie from the list and pick a translation.

After the movie will start, during the opening credits,
click on the "Start" button and wait while the application finish synchronization.
Usually takes about 10 to 20 seconds. When the synchronization finishes you will hear the dialog translations in the headphones.

Note! It is important keep relative silence at the time of synchronization process.
