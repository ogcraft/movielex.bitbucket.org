We are proud to present MovieLexApp. MovieLexApp is a smartphone app that enables non-English speaking audiences to enjoy original mainstream english speaking movies in theaters and on home TV sets, by allowing them to hear the corresponding audio track translated to their own language, in real time, via headphones connected to their smartphone.

As first phase MovieLexApp provides Russian translation for English language movies later we are planning to add more languages and movies.

The list of currently available movies you can find here.

How it works?

The user selects the movie from the list provided by application and downloads from the site movie translation track.
After the movie begins the user press the "Start" button. The application uses the smartphone microphone to synchronize the translation audio track with of ongoing movie. After the application finish synchronization process, it usually take about 10-20 seconds, the user can hear in his headphones translation of movie dialogs.

Try a demo

We offer you a short excerpt from the film, which you can try to see how it works in practice.

To do this you need to install the "MovieLexApp" on your smartphone, choose "MovieLex demo" film from the list and download translation data.

Then click on the "LISTEN" button.

At the beginning of the film, during the opening credits, click on the "Start" button and wait while the application finish synchronization. It takes about 10 to 20 seconds. When the synchronization finishes you will hear the dialog translations in the headphones.

It is important at the time of synchronization to sat in relative silence.
